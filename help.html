Help

  • 'Use By' indicates that the food is unsafe to eat after the date specified. This is put on for health and safety reasons. You should never eat food past its 'Use By' date.

    'Best Before' indicates the time when the food is at its freshest, but the food won't necessarily be unsafe after this date. You should still take care before eating food past its 'Best Before' date, as it may still be unsafe.
